Recipe Name
Onion Garlic Chicken
Recipe Ingredients
Chicken Breast
Onion Powder
Garlic Powder
Seasoning Salt
Butter
Frying Pan
Recipe Steps
Lay chicken breast on cutting board and season both sides with a coat of onion, garlic, and seasoning salt
Melt about 3 tablespoons of butter in frying pan
Add chicken to melted butter in frying pan and cook both sides for 10-15 minutes on medium heat
Slice chicken and serve
